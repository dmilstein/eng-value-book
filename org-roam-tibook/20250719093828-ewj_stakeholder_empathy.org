#+title: EWJ Stakeholder Empathy
- who do they report to?
- how does that person judge them?
- what do they consider themselves responsible for?
- where does that mismatch with their job title / description?
- how do they consider themselves a hero (i.e. what do they think they do that isn't acknowledged / recognized that's "keeping everything together"?)
- what are they afraid of?
- what are they unhappy about?
- what job do you think they ultimately want?
- what do they think you're doing wrong / not doing?
